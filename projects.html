Projects I'm Working On

I'll never finish any of these.

Pi Peep Hole

The goal here is to make a cyberpunk aesthetic peephole using a RPi Zero and camera module coupled with a tv to give a live feed of the view through my peephole. I've gone through a few iterations, and I am close to being to where I can call it "useable" but a finished state still eludes me.

High Memory iPod Classic

For those of you who haven't heard of or seen anything from DankPods on YouTube he has a fantastic channel where he restores and improves classic iPods. Since I'm apparently easily swayed by YouTube videos, I've decided to do the same to an iPod I purchased off eBay. Thankfully I bought one when I did because thanks to DankPods, the cost of secondhand iPods has skyrocketed recently. The goal is to have the iPod sit at either 500 GB or 1TB. I haven't decided yet. I have a sizeable collection of FLAC files that are rather large so I'll have to decide if I want them all on the iPod.

X1 Carbon 2nd Gen Repair

Recently I was fortunate enough to come into possession of a 2nd Generation ThinkPad X1 Carbon laptop. However the top shell is cracked and the screen may be damaged. I've also noticed some scuffs around the edges as if the device has been dropped. My plan is to procure a charger for the device and figure out how deep the damage really goes, then repair it. Thankfully it shouldn't be too difficult as long as I don't have to resolder any ridiculously tiny components or anything. The ThinkPad community online has fantastic resources for projects such as this.

Game Console Modifications

Last year my YouTube suggestions introduced me to the wonderful channel of Voultar who, doesn't post much, but the quality is great. Again, my impressionable self was convinced to begin modding my old consoles, and so far I've only done one mod but it was very satisfying.

The first, and only, mod I've done was to add a Mayumi Mod Chip into my SCPH-7501 PS1 console. The solder isn't the cleanest but it works, and I'll probably revisit it down the road. I have planned though, for my other PS1, to add in an ODE and digital output mods for play on an HDTV without the need for something like an OSSC and discs. I also intend to do the same with my Dreamcast and possibly in the future a PS2.
