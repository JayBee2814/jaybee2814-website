Personal Projects/Hobbies

Cooking and being in the kitchen is a passion of mine. Given the chance, I'll cook for myself or other people 99 times out of 100. My favorite cuisines to work with are Italian and Japanese, however I also greatly enjoy to make a variety of dishes from the African and European Continents.

Gunpla

Gunpla, or Gundam Plastic Models, is the art of building and customizing the various model kits of the Mobile Suit Gundam franchise as produced by Bandai. It's a deeply rewarding hobby that flexes your creativity and hand eye coordination. In my spare time I can sometimes be found building new kits, trying to design custom color schemes for them or planning out dioramas. Currently I have 2 display cases full of kits, and will almost certainly be adding a third case fairly soon.
